Industry Leadership

Laura Thompson and Shilpa Smith have delivered accredited weight management qualifications, led communities of practice, spearheaded the latest renewal of the 11407NAT Certificate IV in Weight Management, maintained scope of practice frameworks and collaborated with leading medical, allied health and industry stakeholders to advance wellness education and professional standards across Australia.
