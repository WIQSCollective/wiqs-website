About WIQS Collective

WIQS Collective was established to support excellence across the wellness sector through quality-assured education, workforce development, industry consultation and professional standards.

Led by Directors Laura Thompson and Shilpa Smith, WIQS draws on more than 40 years of combined experience across the Registered Training Organisation sector, including compliance, quality systems, accredited course development, assessment, implementation and continuous improvement.

Our focus extends beyond qualifications. We are committed to building industry capability, supporting quality outcomes and contributing to robust standards within emerging health and wellbeing disciplines.
