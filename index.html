About WIQS Collective

WIQS Collective exists to support excellence in wellness education through quality assurance, industry consultation, accredited education products and continuous improvement.

Led by Directors Laura Thompson and Shilpa Smith, WIQS brings more than 40 years of combined experience across the Registered Training Organisation sector.
